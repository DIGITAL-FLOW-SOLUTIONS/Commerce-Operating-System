MERCHANT DASHBOARD (/~)

🧠 🎯 PRIMARY PURPOSE

The Merchant Dashboard is:

The central operating system interface where every merchant manages their commerce activities across all surfaces (TikTok, Boutique, Payments, Stores, Subscriptions).
